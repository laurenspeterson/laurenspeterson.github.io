Idaho Article of the Week

With the COVID-19 virus trapping the world in their homes, Idahoans have been coming up with ways to entertain themselves. With no one worried about much of a food shortage, due to insane amounts of potatoes, people have actually started using potatoes as one of the main sources of entertainment. For kids that are staying home from school, potatoes have proven to be a useful tool in their at home schooling. They have used them for carving shapes, and making homeade science projects, and playing games like "hot potato". Other kids have even taken the potato and recreated the once famous Mr. Potato head. We want to hear from you! What are ways you have had fun this during this quarantine?
