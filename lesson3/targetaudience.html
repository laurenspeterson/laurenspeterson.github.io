Personas And Target Audience

The target audience is anyone who lives in the area that is shown on this website and anuone that amy be visiting the area. People that are interested in the weather.



Target Audience

Persona 1

A group of high school students that are planning a hike with their friends and need to know what day the weather will be the best.

Persona 2

A stay at home mom with her 3 kids that love playing outside and going to the neighborhood pool. They need to know the weather so they know what day they should plan a pool day.



Scenarios
  • Will it rain this saturday?
  • What day will this week will be warmest for a hike?
  • Will it rain tomorrow during our neighborhood pool party?
  • What is the High for Wednesday?
  • What's the weather going to be this weekend at the trail I'm planning to hike?
